Frequently Asked Questions

What is Attendify and who is it for?

Attendify is a membership and attendance manager for gyms, fitness studios, yoga and martial arts schools, dance academies, and coaching centers. It replaces paper registers with a simple app that works on any smartphone.

How much does Attendify cost? Is there a free version?

Start on the free tier for core tracking. As you grow, paid plans unlock analytics, automated reminders, and multi-location support at a low monthly rate.

How does attendance tracking work?

Members scan a QR code when they arrive. Check-ins are time-stamped automatically, and you can view daily, weekly, or monthly attendance from anywhere.

Can I import my existing member data from Excel?

Yes. Upload CSV or Excel files and migrate your member database without losing information. We can guide you during onboarding.

Does Attendify send automatic renewal reminders?

Attendify tracks expiry dates and sends reminders via SMS, Email, or WhatsApp before memberships lapse. That reduces churn and saves manual follow-up.

Is my member data secure?

Data is encrypted, stored on secure cloud infrastructure, and never shared with third parties. You retain full ownership of your member information.

Can I manage multiple locations?

Yes. Multi-location management lets you see performance across branches or drill into each site from a single dashboard.

What makes Attendify different?

It’s built specifically for recurring membership businesses, not generic time-tracking. You get attendance, renewals, reminders, analytics, and communication in one place without bloat.
