Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about how we work and what to expect

How much does a custom website cost in the USA?

Full-stack, research-backed builds that include strategy, copy support, UX, development, QA, and launch start at $200 USD and can go up to $20,000 USD based on scope. We size each proposal to the outcomes you want—more qualified leads, faster speed, or better conversion—so you know exactly why the investment makes sense.

Why might my current site not be generating leads?

Most underperforming sites hide the value: unclear calls to action, slow pages, weak messaging, and little proof. We rebuild around your buyers—clear offers, fast performance, trust signals, and forms placed where intent is highest—so visitors know what to do next.

What’s the difference between custom and template sites?

Templates are shared and rigid—they’re quick but often slow, generic, and tough to scale. Custom builds start with your goals, brand, and funnel, so we control speed, UX, SEO, and analytics from day one. The result is a site that feels yours, loads fast, and converts.

How long does it take to build?

Typical delivery is 14 days from kickoff. We map discovery, design approval, build, QA, and launch into clear milestones and keep you updated so you always know what’s next.

Do you work with clients in the USA, UK, Canada & Australia?

Yes. We regularly work with businesses across the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, and Europe. Our process is fully remote-friendly with async milestone updates, timezone-flexible calls, and clear deliverables at every stage.

What’s included in your website projects?

Strategy, custom UX/UI, responsive build, on-page SEO, analytics, conversion tracking, AI search optimization, lead forms, and post-launch support. We also handle performance, accessibility, and QA so you launch with confidence.

How do you optimize for Google and AI search?

We pair technical SEO (speed, structure, schema) with clear answers to buyer questions. For AI search, we use structured content and consistent facts so assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity can surface accurate recommendations.

What is AI search optimization (LLMO)?

LLMO makes your business easy for AI assistants to understand and recommend. We structure your offers, location, proof, and services so large language models return you as a trusted option when people ask for help.

What do you need from us to start?

Brand basics (logo, colors), your audience, and the outcomes you want. If you have copy or examples you like, great—if not, we guide you. Kickoff is a short call to align on goals and priorities.

What support do you provide after launch?

We stay for post-launch fixes and tuning, then offer optional maintenance. You also get documentation so your team can handle routine updates without being locked in.

Do you build SaaS platforms, web apps & mobile apps?

Yes. Beyond marketing websites, AiVirex builds custom web applications, SaaS platforms (MVP to full-scale), and mobile apps for iOS and Android. We handle architecture, development, and deployment end-to-end — from idea to a live product.

Is AiVirex better value than hiring a local agency or freelancer?

AiVirex delivers agency-quality work — a full team of researcher, designer, developer, and SEO specialist — at a fraction of US/UK agency rates. Unlike freelancers, you get guaranteed timelines and accountability. Unlike traditional agencies, no hourly billing or 3-month waits. Fixed price. 14 days. Done.
